1 Elemental Advantage System

  • Fire beats Ice: Fire attacks deal 150% damage to ice dragons
  • Ice beats Nature: Ice attacks are super effective against nature
  • Nature beats Earth: Nature dragons overpower earth elements
  • Earth beats Thunder: Earth absorbs lightning attacks
  • Thunder beats Fire: Lightning overwhelms fire defenses

💡 Element Strategy

  • Always bring heroes matching the dragon's weakness
  • Mixed element teams work well against legendary dragons
  • Check dragon element before starting the battle

Team Composition for Dragons

Building the right team is essential for surviving dragon attacks and dealing maximum damage.

1 Recommended Team Setup

  • 1 Tank Hero: Absorbs dragon's main attacks (Alexander, Marcus)
  • 2 DPS Heroes: Deal consistent damage (Leonidas, Artemis)
  • 1 Healer: Keeps team alive through battle (Luna, Cleopatra)
  • 1 Flex Slot: Additional DPS or utility based on dragon type

Example Team vs Fire Dragon:

  • Marcus (Tank) - absorbs damage
  • Artemis (DPS) - water element bonus
  • Leonidas (DPS) - sustained damage
  • Luna (Healer) - team sustainability

2 Hero Skills Priority

  • Tank Skills: Maximize defense and taunt abilities
  • DPS Skills: Focus on single-target damage skills
  • Healer Skills: Prioritize healing power and mana regen
  • Ultimate Timing: Save ultimates for dragon's big attacks

3 Troop Requirements

  • Tier Level: Higher tier troops survive better
  • Troop Count: Bring full army (don't skimp on troops)
  • Troop Type: Mixed composition works best
  • Healing: Ensure hospital has capacity for wounded

⚠️ Important

Dragon battles can result in heavy troop losses if you're underprepared. Always check recommended power level before attempting!

Battle Mechanics & Patterns

1 Understanding Dragon Attacks

  • Basic Attack: Regular damage to frontline troops
  • Special Attack: Powerful AoE attack with cooldown
  • Enrage Phase: Dragon gets stronger at low HP
  • Wipe Mechanic: Some dragons have instant-kill attacks
  • Pattern Recognition: Learn attack patterns to anticipate

2 Battle Phases

  • Phase 1 (100-70% HP): Dragon uses basic attacks mostly
  • Phase 2 (70-30% HP): Special attacks become frequent
  • Phase 3 (30-0% HP): Enrage mode, devastating attacks

Strategy per Phase:

  • Phase 1: Build up energy, conserve ultimates
  • Phase 2: Use healing when special attacks hit
  • Phase 3: Go all-out with ultimates and buffs

3 Surviving Dragon Attacks

  • Positioning: Keep healer protected behind tank
  • Timing: Activate defensive skills before big attacks
  • Recovery: Heal immediately after heavy damage
  • Retreat Option: Some battles allow retreat to regroup
