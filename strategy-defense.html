Defense Strategy Guide

Protect your kingdom from enemy attacks with optimal defense setups. Learn wall placement, troop positioning, hero formations, and advanced defensive tactics.

City Layout & Wall Placement

A well-designed city layout is your first line of defense. Proper wall placement can significantly delay attackers and minimize losses.

1 Core Protection Principle

The fundamental rule of city defense: protect your most valuable buildings at all costs.

  • Town Hall: Always place at the absolute center
  • Warehouse: Keep near Town Hall to protect resources
  • Hospital: Essential for troop recovery, protect it
  • Research Lab: High priority target, keep safe
  • Barracks: Can be outside core but still protected

2 Wall Layering Strategy

Multiple wall layers create choke points and slow down attackers.

  • Outer Wall: First layer to absorb initial damage
  • Middle Wall: Second layer protecting core buildings
  • Inner Wall: Final barrier around Town Hall
  • Gap Management: Leave strategic gaps to funnel enemies
  • Wall Level: Prioritize upgrading walls evenly

💡 Wall Upgrade Tip

  • Don't over-invest in walls early - focus on Town Hall first
  • Upgrade walls when you have excess stone resources
  • Higher level walls dramatically increase defense time

3 Defensive Buildings

  • Arrow Towers: Deal ranged damage to approaching troops
  • Cannon Towers: Area damage effective against grouped units
  • Mage Towers: Magic damage ignores some armor
  • Traps: Hidden damage sources that surprise attackers
  • Buffs: Activate defensive buffs before logging off

Troop Positioning & Garrison

How you position your troops determines how effectively they can defend your city.

1 Garrison Composition

Your garrison army should be optimized for defense, not offense.

  • 70% Infantry: Tanks absorb damage and hold the line
  • 20% Rangers: Ranged DPS from protected positions
  • 10% Cavalry: Counter-attack capability if walls break
  • Total Troops: Fill garrison to maximum capacity
  • Troop Level: Higher level troops defend much better

2 Hero Assignment for Defense

Choose the right heroes to lead your garrison defense.

  • Primary Defender: Use your highest DEF stat hero (Marcus, Alexander)
  • Secondary Hero: Add a support hero with healing or buffs (Luna, Cleopatra)
  • Skill Priority: Maximize defensive skills and passive buffs
  • Hero Level: Keep garrison heroes well-leveled
  • Equipment: Equip defensive gear on garrison heroes

💡 Best Defensive Heroes

  • Marcus: Highest DEF among Rare heroes, excellent for F2P
  • Alexander: Legendary tank with powerful defensive abilities
  • Caesar: Good balance of defense and counter-attack

3 Reinforcement Strategy

  • Alliance Reinforcements: Request troops from alliance members
  • Reinforcement Capacity: Upgrade castle to hold more reinforcements
  • Troop Variety: Different reinforcement types add flexibility
  • Active Coordination: Coordinate with online allies during attacks

Shield Timing & Protection

Understanding when and how to use shields is crucial for protecting your progress.

1 Shield Types

  • Beginner Shield: Automatic for new players (expires at certain power level)
  • Peace Shield: Purchased or earned, provides timed protection
  • Alliance Shield: Some alliances provide collective shields
  • Event Shield: Special event rewards may include shields

2 When to Activate Shields

  • Long Offline Periods: Always shield before sleeping or extended absence
  • After Major Battles: Shield when your army is weakened
  • During Wars: Keep shield active during active conflict periods
  • High Resource Storage: Shield when carrying lots of unprotected resources
  • Vulnerable Position: Shield if surrounded by hostile players

⚠️ Important

Shields expire even when offline! Always check remaining shield time before logging off. A expired shield leaves you completely vulnerable.

3 Shield Alternatives

  • Teleport Away: Random teleport to escape persistent attackers
  • Alliance Territory: Being in alliance territory provides some protection
  • Zero Resources: Spend all resources so there's nothing to loot
  • Small Target: Stay below radar of major powers

Advanced Defensive Tactics

1 Trap Placement

Strategic trap placement can turn the tide of battle.

  • Entrance Traps: Place at common entry points
  • Core Traps: Protect Town Hall with high-damage traps
  • Decoy Traps: Place near less important buildings as distraction
  • Trap Variety: Mix different trap types for unpredictability

2 Rally Defense

When facing rally attacks from multiple enemies:

  • Request Help Immediately: Alert alliance at first sign of rally
  • Reinforcement Rush: Get allies to send defensive troops
  • Shield Backup: Have shield ready as last resort
  • Counter-Rally: Coordinate alliance counter-attacks

3 Scout Analysis

If you notice scouts checking your city:

  • Increase Defense: Assume attack is imminent
  • Hide Resources: Use warehouse protection or spend resources
  • Alert Alliance: Inform allies of potential threat
  • Prepare Evacuation: Be ready to teleport if needed
