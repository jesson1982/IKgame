General Questions

What should I do first as a beginner?

Complete tutorial quests, join an active alliance early, focus on resource buildings first, don't waste speed-ups randomly, and save items for growth events. Prioritize upgrading your Town Hall to unlock new features.

How do I get more gems?

Gems can be earned through daily quests, events, achievements, and completing campaign levels. You can also purchase them from the shop or receive them as alliance gifts. Participate in kingdom championship for large gem rewards.

Is this game pay-to-win?

While spending money helps, skilled free-to-play players can compete by being strategic, joining strong alliances, and participating actively in events. Time and dedication can compensate for lack of spending.

Combat Questions

What's the best troop type?

Each troop type has strengths: Infantry beats Rangers, Rangers beat Cavalry, Cavalry beats Infantry. A balanced army with all three types is usually best. Adjust composition based on enemy troops.

How do I win battles consistently?

Scout enemies before attacking, use appropriate heroes for your army type, upgrade your technology, coordinate with alliance members, and attack when you have numerical advantage. Timing is crucial.

What's the best hero combination?

The ideal combination depends on your strategy. A balanced team typically includes a tank, DPS dealer, and support/healer. Popular combos include Alexander + Cleopatra + Leonidas for versatile gameplay.

Resource Questions

How do I protect my resources from being stolen?

Use resource protection items, keep resources below warehouse capacity, join an active alliance for reinforcement, activate shields when offline for extended periods, and use farm cities to store excess resources.

Which resource is most important?

Early game: Food and Wood are critical. Mid game: Stone becomes important for upgrades. Late game: Iron is needed for advanced buildings. Balance all resource production rather than focusing on just one.

Should I buy resource packs?

Resource packs are generally good value if you need a quick boost. Wait for special events that offer bonus resources with purchases. Free-to-play players should focus on gathering and production instead.

Hero Questions

Should I spend gold on summoning heroes?

Gold summons are generally not recommended for free-to-play players. Save your gold for special summoning events that offer better rates and guaranteed rewards. Use free scrolls whenever available.

How do I level up heroes faster?

Use XP tomes from events, complete daily quests, participate in dragon hunts, and feed duplicate hero shards. Focus leveling on your main team rather than spreading XP across many heroes.

Which heroes should I prioritize?

Prioritize heroes that fit your playstyle: Tank heroes for defense, DPS heroes for attacking, Support heroes for versatility. Epic heroes are easier to max out than Legendaries and can be very effective.
