Hero Overview

Artemis is the premier Epic Ranger/DPS hero in Infinity Kingdom. With the highest attack stat among epic heroes, Artemis excels at dealing massive ranged damage from the backline. Her bow mastery makes her ideal for players who want pure damage output.

Strengths

  • Highest attack among epic heroes
  • Excellent range advantage
  • Strong single-target burst damage
  • Fast attack speed
  • Great for F2P players

Weaknesses

  • Very fragile without protection
  • Vulnerable to assassins and divers
  • Low HP and defense
  • Requires good positioning

Recommended Builds

Build 1: Glass Cannon (Recommended)

Maximum damage build that turns Artemis into a killing machine.

Equipment

  • Weapon: Hunter's Bow (Attack + Crit)
  • Helmet: Archer's Cap (Attack + Speed)
  • Armor: Light Armor (Attack + Crit Damage)
  • Boots: Swift Boots (Speed + Attack)
  • Ring: Precision Ring (Crit + Penetration)
  • Accessory: Eagle Eye (Range + Crit Rate)

Skill Priority

  1. Ultimate - Arrow Storm: Max first (Multi-shot AOE)
  2. Skill 2 - Piercing Shot: Second (Armor Penetration)
  3. Skill 1 - Quick Shot: Third (Fast Attack)
  4. Passive - Hunter's Mark: Last (Crit Chance)

Battle Strategies

PvE Tips

  • Position Artemis at maximum range
  • Use ultimate to clear adds quickly
  • Focus fire on high-priority targets
  • Keep moving to avoid melee attackers

PvP Tips

  • Target enemy backline first
  • Use terrain to maintain distance
  • Kite melee heroes around obstacles
  • Save ultimate for finishing blows

Player Tips

Tip 1: Artemis is the best F2P DPS hero. Invest heavily in her early game.

Tip 2: Always pair with a strong tank. She cannot survive alone.

Tip 3: Her range advantage is key. Use it to kite enemies.

Tip 4: Crit builds make her devastating. Prioritize crit stats.
