Defensive Buildings Guide

Protect your kingdom with optimal defensive building placement and upgrades. Learn about Walls, Towers, and Traps.

Overview of Defensive Buildings

Defensive buildings are crucial for protecting your resources and city from enemy attacks. Proper placement and upgrades can significantly delay attackers.

🧱 Walls

Walls create barriers that slow down enemy troops and protect core buildings. Multiple wall layers create choke points.

  • Function: Physical barrier against enemy troops
  • Upgrade Benefit: Higher HP, longer destruction time
  • Strategy: Create multiple layers around core buildings
  • Priority: Upgrade evenly, don't over-invest early

💡 Wall Strategy

Create 2-3 wall layers around Town Hall and Warehouse. Leave strategic gaps to funnel enemies into kill zones.

🏹 Arrow Towers

Arrow Towers deal ranged physical damage to approaching enemy troops. Effective against infantry.

  • Damage Type: Physical Ranged
  • Best Against: Infantry units
  • Placement: Behind walls, covering approaches
  • Upgrade Priority: Medium-High

💣 Cannon Towers

Cannon Towers deal area damage effective against grouped enemy units. Great for clearing troop clusters.

  • Damage Type: Area Physical
  • Best Against: Grouped troops
  • Placement: Central positions for maximum coverage
  • Upgrade Priority: High

✨ Mage Towers

Mage Towers deal magic damage that ignores some armor. Effective against heavily armored units.

  • Damage Type: Magic
  • Best Against: Armored cavalry
  • Placement: Mix with other tower types
  • Upgrade Priority: Medium

🕳️ Traps

Traps are hidden defensive structures that activate when enemies step on them. Deal burst damage.

  • Types: Spike Traps, Bomb Traps, Freeze Traps
  • Placement: At common entry points and choke points
  • Strategy: Hide behind walls for surprise factor
  • Upgrade Priority: Low-Medium

Defense Layout Tips

  • Layered Defense: Create multiple defensive rings around core buildings
  • Tower Coverage: Ensure towers can cover wall breach points
  • Trap Placement: Place traps where enemies naturally cluster
  • Balance: Mix different tower types for versatility
  • Upgrade Evenly: Don't max one tower type while neglecting others
