Understanding KvK Warfare

Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK) is the ultimate test of coordination, strategy, and power in Infinity Kingdom. Success requires more than individual strength—it demands kingdom-wide cooperation.

1 What is KvK?

  • Cross-Server Battles: Your kingdom fights against other kingdoms
  • Seasonal Events: KvK seasons run for specific periods
  • Territory Control: Capture and hold strategic locations
  • Fortress Battles: Major objectives provide kingdom-wide bonuses
  • Ranking System: Kingdoms earn rewards based on performance
  • Individual Rewards: Personal contributions earn exclusive items

2 KvK Phases

  • Preparation Phase: Build up resources, train troops, coordinate strategies
  • Qualification Phase: Kingdoms compete to enter main KvK bracket
  • Group Stage: Multiple kingdoms battle in groups
  • Elimination Stage: Top kingdoms face off in knockout rounds
  • Finals: Championship battle for ultimate glory

💡 Preparation is Key

  • Use preparation phase to stockpile resources and troops
  • Coordinate with alliance on target priorities
  • Ensure all members understand their roles

1 Fortress Types

  • Border Fortresses: Entry points, easier to capture
  • Inner Fortresses: Strategic positions, moderate difficulty
  • Central Fortress: High value, heavily contested
  • Throne Room: Ultimate objective, requires kingdom coordination

2 Capture Mechanics

  • Rally Attacks: Multiple players must rally the fortress
  • Hold Time: Must maintain control for set duration
  • Defense: Enemy can counter-attack to reclaim
  • Garrison: Station troops to increase hold strength
  • Supply Lines: Connected territories provide bonuses

3 Fortress Attack Strategy

  • Scout First: Check enemy garrison strength
  • Coordinate Timing: Multiple rallies hit simultaneously
  • Overwhelming Force: Bring more power than defender
  • Follow-Up Defense: Immediately reinforce after capture
  • Supply Route: Maintain connection to friendly territory

⚠️ Common Mistake

Capturing a fortress without securing supply lines makes it vulnerable to easy recapture. Always ensure connected territory before pushing deeper!

Territory Control & Supply Lines

1 Territory Basics

  • Connected Tiles: Territories must connect to expand
  • Supply Lines: Broken connections weaken held positions
  • Choke Points: Narrow passages are critical to control
  • Resource Tiles: Provide ongoing benefits to holder
  • Vision: Controlled territory reveals enemy movements

2 Expansion Strategy

  • Secure Flanks: Protect sides before pushing forward
  • Gradual Advance: Don't overextend supply lines
  • Key Positions: Prioritize strategically important tiles
  • Defensive Perimeter: Establish defensible borders
  • Retreat Routes: Always have fallback positions planned

3 Cutting Enemy Supply

  • Identify Weak Points: Find thin connections in enemy territory
  • Flank Attacks: Hit supply lines from unexpected angles
  • Simultaneous Strikes: Cut multiple points at once
  • Hold the Cut: Maintain pressure on severed connections
  • Exploit Chaos: Push advantage when enemy is disrupted

💡 Advanced Tactic

  • Cutting supply lines isolates enemy fortresses
  • Isolated fortresses are much easier to recapture
  • Coordinate with team to execute supply cuts effectively

Cross-Server Strategy & Communication

1 Kingdom-Wide Coordination

  • Leadership Structure: Clear chain of command essential
  • Communication Channels: Discord or similar for real-time coordination
  • Role Assignment: Each player knows their responsibilities
  • Target Calls: Leaders announce priority targets
  • Response Time: Quick reaction to changing situations

2 Alliance Cooperation

  • Cross-Alliance Teams: Work with other alliances in your kingdom
  • Resource Sharing: Help weaker kingdom members prepare
  • Joint Rallies: Coordinate multi-alliance attacks
  • Intel Sharing: Share scout reports and enemy info
  • Unified Strategy: All alliances follow same game plan

3 Battle Timeline Management

  • Schedule Conflicts: Plan around peak activity times
  • Time Zones: Consider international player base
  • Shift System: Rotate active defense/offense shifts
  • Emergency Protocol: Quick response plan for surprise attacks
  • Rest Periods: Ensure players get adequate rest

Advanced KvK Tactics

1 Bait & Switch

  • Weak Target: Appear vulnerable at one location
  • Trap Setup: Hide strong forces nearby
  • Ambush: Counter-attack when enemy commits
  • Quick Strike: Hit exposed enemy positions while they're distracted

2 Multi-Front Pressure

  • Simultaneous Attacks: Strike multiple locations at once
  • Stretch Defenses: Force enemy to split their forces
  • Exploit Weakness: Punch through weakest defended area
  • Maintain Pressure: Keep enemy reacting, not acting

3 Economic Warfare

  • Resource Denial: Capture enemy resource tiles
  • Gathering Disruption: Kill enemy gathering armies
  • Trade Blockade: Control key trade routes
  • War of Attrition: Drain enemy resources through constant pressure

4 Endgame Strategy

  • Throne Push: Final assault on throne room
  • All-In Timing: Coordinate kingdom-wide final push
  • Reserve Deployment: Commit remaining forces
  • Victory Conditions: Hold throne until timer expires
  • Defensive Setup: Prepare for desperate enemy counter-attacks
