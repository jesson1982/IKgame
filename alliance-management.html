Alliance Management Guide

Build and lead successful alliances with effective recruitment, communication, and war coordination strategies.

Recruitment Strategy

Finding Quality Members

  • Target Active Players: Look for players who log in daily and participate in events
  • Check Activity History: Verify login frequency before sending invites
  • Power Requirements: Set minimum power levels based on alliance goals
  • Language Match: Recruit players who speak the same language for better coordination

Retention Strategies

  • Welcoming Atmosphere: Greet new members and help them settle in
  • Clear Expectations: Communicate activity requirements and alliance rules
  • Regular Events: Organize alliance activities to build community
  • Fair Leadership: Treat all members equally and resolve conflicts quickly
  • Growth Support: Help weaker members improve through advice and reinforcements

💡 Quality Over Quantity

A smaller active alliance outperforms a large inactive one. Focus on retaining 50 dedicated members rather than 100 inactive ones.

War Coordination

Rally Organization

  • Designate Rally Leaders: Choose experienced players to start rallies
  • Set Rally Times: Coordinate when rallies will launch
  • Communicate Targets: Clearly announce which target to attack
  • Troop Composition: Advise members on optimal troop types

Defense Coordination

  • Reinforcement System: Request defensive troops from members
  • Shield Rotation: Coordinate shield activation times
  • Scout Network: Share intel about enemy movements
  • Emergency Protocol: Have quick response plan for surprise attacks

Alliance Technology

Alliance Tech provides permanent bonuses to all members. Contribute regularly to accelerate research.

  • Economy Tech: Increases resource production and gathering speed
  • Military Tech: Boosts troop attack and defense stats
  • Defense Tech: Improves city defense and wall HP
  • Contribution Priority: Focus on economy first, then military
  • Daily Donations: Contribute maximum allowed daily

💡 Tech Strategy

Coordinate with alliance leadership to focus donations on specific tech trees. Unified effort accelerates progress significantly.
