Resource Farming Guide

Efficient methods to gather and manage resources for rapid kingdom growth. Master farm cities, gathering routes, trade optimization, and resource protection.

Resource Types & Usage

Understanding each resource type and its primary uses helps you prioritize farming efforts.

Resource Primary Uses Farm Priority
🌾Food Troop training, feeding population, healing Highest - Always needed
🪵Wood Building upgrades, research, troop training High - Early game critical
🪨Stone Advanced buildings, walls, high-tier research Medium - Mid-late game
💎Gold Premium currency, speed-ups, special items Variable - Don't overspend

1 Resource Production Buildings

  • Farm: Produces food continuously
  • Lumber Mill: Produces wood over time
  • Quarry: Generates stone resources
  • Gold Mine: Slowly produces gold
  • Upgrade Priority: Keep production buildings at Town Hall level

2 Storage Management

  • Warehouse: Protects portion of resources from raids
  • Protection Limit: Resources below warehouse capacity are safe
  • Overflow: Resources above limit are vulnerable to theft
  • Strategy: Spend or store excess resources before logging off

💡 Pro Tip

  • Keep resources just below warehouse protection limit when offline
  • Use excess resources for upgrades or training before logging off
  • Consider creating a "farm city" to store extra resources safely

Farm City Setup

Creating dedicated farm cities is one of the most effective ways to boost resource income.

1 What is a Farm City?

A farm city is a secondary city optimized purely for resource production:

  • Multiple Cities: Most players can unlock multiple city slots
  • Specialization: Each city can focus on different tasks
  • Resource Focus: Farm cities maximize production buildings
  • Minimal Military: Little to no military investment needed
  • Safe Location: Place in alliance territory for protection

2 Setting Up Your Farm City

  • Location: Choose a spot near your main city or in alliance territory
  • Building Priority: Max out all resource production buildings
  • Research: Focus on economy and gathering technologies
  • Heroes: Assign heroes with gathering bonuses
  • Defense: Minimal - rely on location and shields for protection
  • Transfers: Use resource transfer items to move goods to main city

💡 Farm City Strategy

  • Create separate farm cities for each resource type
  • One city focused on food, another on wood, etc.
  • This specialization maximizes efficiency and hero bonuses

3 Farm City Protection

  • Alliance Territory: Build within alliance borders for bonus protection
  • Shield Rotation: Keep shield active when not actively using
  • Low Profile: Don't make farm cities obvious targets
  • Reinforcements: Station some defensive troops from main city

Gathering Routes Optimization

Efficient gathering from world map tiles significantly boosts resource income.

1 Finding Good Tiles

  • Tile Level: Higher level tiles give more resources
  • Distance: Closer tiles mean faster march times
  • Resource Type: Match tile to your current needs
  • Competition: Avoid heavily contested tiles
  • Safety: Check for enemy players nearby

2 Gathering Heroes

Certain heroes provide significant gathering bonuses:

  • Gathering Skills: Look for heroes with +Gathering Speed
  • Load Capacity: Some heroes increase carrying capacity
  • March Speed: Faster heroes reach tiles quicker
  • Recommended: Assign dedicated gathering heroes and keep them rested

3 Efficient Gathering Routine

  • Continuous Marches: Always have armies gathering
  • Auto-Gather: Use auto-gather feature for convenience
  • Tile Hopping: Move to fresh tiles when current depletes
  • Event Bonus: Gather during gathering events for multipliers
  • Alliance Help: Request help for faster gathering

💡 Gathering Efficiency

  • Set gathering marches before logging off for passive income
  • Use speed-ups on long gathering marches for efficiency
  • Coordinate with alliance to share good tile locations

Trade & Marketplace Optimization

1 Using the Marketplace

  • Surplus Trading: Sell excess resources you don't need
  • Buying Deficits: Purchase resources you're lacking
  • Exchange Rates: Watch for favorable exchange rates
  • Daily Limits: Maximize daily trade limits
  • Alliance Members: Trade with allies for better rates

2 Trade Best Practices

  • Never trade at unfavorable rates unless urgent
  • Stockpile resources when prices are low
  • Sell during high demand periods
  • Use gold wisely - don't waste on common resources
  • Check marketplace multiple times daily for deals

Event-Based Resource Farming

1 Gathering Events

  • Bonus Multipliers: Events often provide 2x-5x gathering bonuses
  • Preparation: Save energy and troops for these events
  • Maximize Time: Gather continuously during event periods
  • Rewards: Complete event tasks for additional resource packages

2 Other Resource Events

  • Login Rewards: Daily login often gives resource packs
  • Achievement Rewards: Complete achievements for lump sums
  • Alliance Gifts: Active alliances distribute resource gifts
  • Seasonal Events: Special events offer unique resource opportunities
