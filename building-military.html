Military Buildings Guide

Train and upgrade your army with these essential military structures. Learn optimal upgrade priorities and troop training strategies.

Overview of Military Buildings

Military buildings are the backbone of your army production. Upgrading them unlocks higher tier troops and increases training capacity.

🏛️ Barracks

The Barracks is where you train infantry troops. Infantry forms the frontline of your army with high defense and HP.

Upgrade Benefits:

  • Unlock higher tier infantry (T2 at level 5, T3 at level 10, etc.)
  • Increase training queue capacity
  • Reduce training time
  • Increase infantry attack and defense stats

💡 Pro Tip

Always keep your Barracks upgrading! Higher tier infantry dramatically outperform lower tiers. Focus on reaching T4/T5 as soon as possible.

Troop Tier Progression

Understanding troop tiers helps you plan your building upgrades strategically.

  • T1 Troops: Basic units, unlocked at building level 1. Weak but cheap.
  • T2 Troops: Unlocked at level 5. Moderate improvement over T1.
  • T3 Troops: Unlocked at level 10. Significant power jump. Good for mid-game.
  • T4 Troops: Unlocked at level 15. Strong units for serious warfare.
  • T5 Troops: Unlocked at level 20+. Elite units, expensive but devastating.

💡 Tier Strategy

Don't rush to T5 if you can't afford it! T4 armies are more cost-effective for most players. Only upgrade to T5 when you have strong resource production.
