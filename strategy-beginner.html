Beginner's Guide

Essential tips and strategies for new players starting their journey in Infinity Kingdom. Master the basics and build a strong foundation for your kingdom.

First Week Priorities

Your first week in Infinity Kingdom sets the foundation for your entire gameplay experience. Focus on these critical tasks to maximize your early progress.

1 Complete Tutorial & Main Quests

The tutorial and main questline provide essential rewards and unlock core features. Don't skip any steps!

  • Main Quest Priority: Always prioritize main quests over side activities
  • Reward Collection: Claim all quest rewards immediately
  • Feature Unlocks: Each completed quest unlocks new buildings and features
  • Resource Boost: Early quests give substantial resource packages

💡 Pro Tip

  • Don't start secondary activities until you've exhausted all available main quests
  • Keep an eye on quest requirements and prepare resources in advance

2 City Building Fundamentals

Proper city layout and upgrade priority is crucial for efficient growth.

  • Town Hall First: Always upgrade your Town Hall before other buildings
  • Resource Buildings: Upgrade farms, mines, and lumber mills to level 10 early
  • Storage Capacity: Keep warehouses upgraded to protect resources
  • Military Buildings: Build barracks and training grounds after resource infrastructure
  • Research Lab: Start researching economy tech immediately

⚠️ Common Mistake

Many new players focus too much on military buildings early on. Remember: without strong resource production, you can't sustain a large army!

3 Resource Management Basics

Understanding resource flow is key to steady progression.

  • Four Main Resources: Food, Wood, Stone, and Gold each serve different purposes
  • Production vs Storage: Balance production rate with storage capacity
  • Resource Protection: Keep resources below warehouse protection limits when offline
  • Trade Wisely: Use the marketplace to convert surplus resources
  • Daily Collection: Collect resource tiles on the world map daily

Army Composition Guide

Building a balanced army is essential for both defense and offense. Here's how to structure your forces as a beginner.

1 Troop Types Overview

  • Infantry: High defense, good for tanking damage. Best for beginners.
  • Cavalry: Fast movement, high attack. Effective against rangers.
  • Rangers: Ranged damage, fragile. Strong against infantry.
  • Siege: Specialized for attacking buildings. Unlock later in the game.

2 Recommended Beginner Army

Start with this composition and adjust as you unlock more heroes:

  • 50% Infantry: Your frontline tanks (use Marcus or similar tank heroes)
  • 30% Rangers: Your main damage dealers (Artemis works well)
  • 20% Cavalry: Flanking and pursuit (Gareth can fill this role)

💡 Army Building Tip

  • Focus on one troop type initially to maximize research benefits
  • Gradually diversify as you acquire more heroes and unlock advanced tech

3 Hero Assignment

Assign the right heroes to lead your armies:

  • Garrison Hero: Use your best defensive hero (high DEF stats)
  • March Leader: Use your highest ATK hero for attacks
  • Gathering Hero: Assign heroes with gathering bonuses to resource runs
  • Reserve Heroes: Keep at least 2 heroes rested for emergency defense

Alliance & Social Features

Joining an alliance early is one of the most important decisions for new players.

1 Why Join an Alliance?

  • Protection: Alliance members can reinforce your city when attacked
  • Bonuses: Earn passive resource and speed boosts
  • Alliance Shop: Access exclusive items and heroes
  • Events: Participate in alliance-specific events for big rewards
  • Knowledge: Learn from experienced players
  • Territory: Benefit from alliance territory bonuses

2 Choosing the Right Alliance

  • Activity Level: Look for alliances with active daily chat and participation
  • Power Range: Join an alliance matching your power level
  • Language: Choose an alliance that speaks your language
  • Requirements: Check if they have minimum power requirements
  • Leadership: Active leaders indicate a healthy alliance

⚠️ Warning

Avoid inactive alliances! Being in a dead alliance is worse than being solo. Look for alliances with recent activity and helpful members.

3 Alliance Contribution

  • Daily Donations: Contribute resources daily to earn alliance currency
  • Alliance Help: Help members with construction and research
  • Territory Building: Construct alliance buildings in territory
  • Event Participation: Join alliance events whenever possible

Hero Summoning Strategy

Smart summoning practices can save you resources and get you better heroes.

1 Save Your Scrolls!

The biggest mistake beginners make is summoning heroes immediately. Here's the smart approach:

  • Event Summoning: Wait for special summoning events with bonus rates
  • Guaranteed Pulls: Some events guarantee epic or legendary heroes
  • Accumulate Scrolls: Save at least 50-100 scrolls before major events
  • Free Summons: Use daily free summons regularly

💡 Summoning Strategy

  • Never spend gems on regular summons - save them for special events
  • Check the summoning calendar and plan your pulls accordingly
  • Focus on getting specific heroes you need rather than random pulling

2 Hero Priority for Beginners

  • First Tank: Prioritize getting a solid tank hero (Marcus, Alexander)
  • DPS Hero: Get at least one reliable damage dealer (Leonidas, Artemis)
  • Support Hero: A healer or buffer is valuable (Luna, Cleopatra)
  • Versatile Heroes: Focus on heroes useful in multiple situations
