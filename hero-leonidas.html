Hero Overview

Leonidas is a powerful Epic Warrior/DPS hero in Infinity Kingdom. As one of the best epic heroes available, Leonidas offers excellent damage output with decent survivability. His Spartan-inspired kit makes him perfect for players who want strong DPS without needing legendary heroes.

Strengths

  • High damage output for an epic hero
  • Easier to obtain and upgrade than legendaries
  • Good synergy with warrior teams
  • Solid survivability for a DPS
  • Excellent value for free-to-play players

Weaknesses

  • Lower ceiling than legendary DPS heroes
  • Requires star levels to compete
  • Vulnerable to focused fire
  • Lacks utility compared to specialists

Recommended Builds

Build 1: Maximum DPS (Recommended)

This build maximizes Leonidas' damage potential. Best for PvE and arena offense.

Equipment

  • Weapon: Spartan Spear (Attack + Crit)
  • Helmet: War Helm (Attack + Crit Damage)
  • Armor: Battle Plate (Attack + HP)
  • Boots: Swift Boots (Speed + Attack)
  • Ring: Power Ring (Attack + Penetration)
  • Accessory: Rage Charm (Crit Rate)

Skill Priority

  1. Ultimate - Spartan Fury: Max first (AOE Damage)
  2. Skill 2 - Phalanx Strike: Second (Single Target Burst)
  3. Skill 1 - Shield Bash: Third (Stun + Damage)
  4. Passive - Warrior Spirit: Last (Attack Boost)

Enchantments

  • Primary: Attack%, Critical Hit%, Crit Damage%
  • Secondary: Warrior Damage%, Penetration

Build 2: Bruiser Build

Balanced build that adds survivability while maintaining good damage.

Equipment

  • Weapon: Royal Sword (Attack + HP)
  • Helmet: Iron Crown (Defense + HP)
  • Armor: Dragon Plate (HP + Defense)
  • Boots: Guardian Boots (Defense + HP)
  • Ring: Life Ring (HP + Regen)
  • Accessory: Protection Amulet (Damage Reduction)

Battle Strategies

PvE (Campaign & Dragons)

  • Use ultimate to clear waves of enemies quickly
  • Focus on single-target skills for boss fights
  • Position behind tanks to avoid direct attacks
  • Pair with healers for sustained damage

PvP (Arena)

  • Target enemy squishy backline heroes
  • Use shield bash to stun key targets
  • Time ultimate after enemy defensive cooldowns
  • Focus fire with your team on one target

Alliance Wars

  • Great secondary rally attacker
  • Use for cleanup after main DPS breaks defenses
  • His warrior buff benefits alliance warriors
  • Cost-effective for multiple march setups

Upgrade Priority

Priority Aspect Reason
High Star Level Epic heroes are easier to star up than legendaries
High Attack Stats Primary role is damage dealing
High Ultimate Skill Main AOE damage ability
Medium Critical Hit Stats Significantly boosts damage output
Low Defense Stats Should be protected by tanks instead

Player Tips

Tip 1: Leonidas is the best epic hero for F2P players. Prioritize his shards over other epics.

Tip 2: He works great as a placeholder until you get legendary DPS heroes.

Tip 3: In warrior teams, Leonidas benefits from multiple synergy bonuses. Always pair with other warriors.

Tip 4: Don't neglect his star level - a 5-star Leonidas can outperform low-star legendaries.
