Economic Buildings Guide

Maximize your resource production with optimal economic building upgrades. Learn about Farms, Lumber Mills, Quarries, and Gold Mines.

Overview of Economic Buildings

Economic buildings generate the resources needed for all activities in Infinity Kingdom. Upgrading them increases production rates and storage capacity.

🌾 Farm

Farms produce food continuously over time. Food is the most consumed resource for training troops and healing.

💡 Pro Tip

Food is always in demand! Keep your Farms at maximum level possible. You can never have too much food production.

Resource Production Tips

  • Balance Production: Don't neglect any resource type completely
  • World Map Gathering: Supplement building production with map gathering
  • Alliance Help: Request resources from alliance when short
  • Marketplace Trading: Convert surplus resources when needed
  • Event Bonuses: Maximize production during resource events

💡 Production Formula

Total Resource Income = Building Production + Map Gathering + Quest Rewards + Alliance Gifts. Diversify your income sources!
