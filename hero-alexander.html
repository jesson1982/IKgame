Hero Overview

Alexander is one of the strongest L Legendary Tank heroes in Infinity Kingdom. Known for his exceptional defensive capabilities and ability to protect his team, Alexander excels at absorbing damage while dealing decent counter-attacks. His kit makes him ideal for both PvE and PvP content.

Strengths

  • Highest HP growth among all heroes
  • Excellent damage reduction skills
  • Strong taunt ability to protect teammates
  • Versatile for all game modes
  • Easy to obtain shards from events

Weaknesses

  • Lower damage output compared to DPS heroes
  • Requires good equipment to maximize potential
  • Vulnerable to true damage
  • Needs high star level for full effectiveness

Recommended Builds

Build 1: Pure Tank (Recommended)

This build maximizes Alexander's survivability, making him an unkillable frontline protector.

Equipment

  • Weapon: Aegis Shield (Defense + HP)
  • Helmet: Iron Crown (HP + Defense)
  • Armor: Dragon Plate (HP + Damage Reduction)
  • Boots: Guardian Steps (Defense + HP)
  • Ring: Life Band (HP + Regen)
  • Accessory: Protection Amulet (Damage Reduction)

Skill Priority

  1. Ultimate - Macedonian Phalanx: Max first (Taunt + Shield)
  2. Skill 2 - Conqueror's Will: Second (Damage Reduction)
  3. Skill 1 - Spear Wall: Third (Counter Damage)
  4. Passive - King's Presence: Last (HP Boost)

Enchantments

  • Primary: HP%, Defense%, Damage Reduction
  • Secondary: Effect Resistance, Healing Received

Build 2: Counter-Attack Tank

Balanced build that allows Alexander to deal meaningful damage while still being tanky.

Equipment

  • Weapon: Royal Spear (Attack + HP)
  • Helmet: War Crown (Attack + Defense)
  • Armor: Battle Plate (HP + Counter Attack)
  • Boots: Swift Boots (Speed + HP)
  • Ring: Power Ring (Attack + Crit)
  • Accessory: Vengeance Charm (Counter Damage)

Battle Strategies

PvE (Dragon Battles & Campaign)

  • Position Alexander in front to absorb dragon attacks
  • Use ultimate when HP drops below 50% for maximum shield value
  • Pair with healers for sustained fights
  • Focus on HP and Defense stats over Attack

PvP (Arena & KvK)

  • Target enemy DPS heroes with taunt
  • Time ultimate to intercept enemy burst damage
  • Use as rally leader for defensive rallies
  • Counter enemy assassin compositions

Alliance Wars

  • Station Alexander in vulnerable cities
  • Lead defensive garrisons with full HP troops
  • Coordinate ultimates with alliance members
  • Use to protect high-value targets

Player Tips

Tip 1: Save your gold summons for Alexander during rate-up events. He's worth investing resources into.

Tip 2: Alexander synergizes well with any DPS hero. Focus on getting him to 5 stars before investing in other tanks.

Tip 3: In KvK, use Alexander to block narrow passages and choke points. His taunt can disrupt enemy formations.

Tip 4: Don't neglect his counter-attack potential. With the right build, he can punish melee attackers.
