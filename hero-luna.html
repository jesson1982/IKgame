Hero Overview

Luna stands out as one of the best Rare healers in Infinity Kingdom, offering exceptional value for F2P and low-spending players. Her dual role as both mage and healer allows her to contribute to damage output while keeping her team healthy throughout battles.

Strengths

  • Excellent healing capabilities for Rare tier
  • Easy to obtain and upgrade (F2P friendly)
  • Good synergy with tank-heavy teams
  • Solid magical damage output
  • Strong early to mid-game performance

Weaknesses

  • Falls off in late game compared to Epic/Legendary healers
  • Vulnerable to burst damage
  • Limited crowd control abilities
  • Requires proper positioning to maximize effectiveness

Combat Strategies

PvE Strategy

In PvE content, position Luna behind your tank to maximize her survivability. Use her healing abilities proactively before taking heavy damage. Focus on upgrading her healing skills first to ensure team sustainability through longer dungeon runs.

PvP Strategy

In PvP, Luna works best when protected by a strong frontline. Time her ultimate ability to counter enemy burst damage. Against high-damage teams, prioritize healing over damage output. In guild wars, she's essential for holding defensive positions.

Player Tips & Tricks

  • Positioning is Key: Always keep Luna behind your tank. She's squishy and will die quickly if targeted by enemy assassins or ranged DPS.
  • Ultimate Timing: Save Luna's ultimate for when your team drops below 50% HP. Don't waste it on minor damage.
  • Early Game Star: Luna shines in early to mid-game. Use her extensively until you unlock better Epic/Legendary healers.
  • Resource Investment: As a Rare hero, Luna is cheap to upgrade. Max her out early for easy progression through campaign stages.
  • Team Synergy: Pair Luna with tanks that have self-heal or damage reduction for maximum sustainability.
