Cleopatra

Legendary

Mage / Support

5/5 - S+ Tier Support
Attack 92/100
Defense 65/100
HP 70/100
Magic 95/100

Hero Overview

Cleopatra is the premier Support/Mage hero in Infinity Kingdom. As the best healer in the game, she provides exceptional healing, powerful buffs to allies, and devastating magic damage. Her versatility makes her essential for any serious team composition.

Strengths

  • Best healing output in the game
  • Powerful attack buff for team
  • High magic damage potential
  • Energy regeneration ability
  • Works with any team composition
  • Excellent in all game modes

Weaknesses

  • Very fragile without protection
  • Requires good positioning
  • Vulnerable to assassins
  • High resource investment needed
  • Needs energy management

Recommended Builds

Build 1: Pure Support (Recommended)

Maximizes Cleopatra's healing and buffing capabilities. Best for PvE and Alliance Wars.

Equipment

  • Weapon: Staff of Ages (Magic + Energy Regen)
  • Helmet: Crown of Wisdom (Magic + HP)
  • Armor: Robe of Healing (Healing Boost + HP)
  • Boots: Swift Sandals (Speed + Energy)
  • Ring: Mana Ring (Magic + Energy Regen)
  • Accessory: Life Amulet (Healing Boost)

Skill Priority

  1. Ultimate - Nile's Blessing: Max first (AOE Heal + Buff)
  2. Skill 2 - Queen's Command: Second (Attack Buff)
  3. Skill 1 - Magic Missile: Third (Damage)
  4. Passive - Royal Blood: Last (Energy Regen)

Enchantments

  • Primary: Healing Boost%, Magic%, Energy Regen
  • Secondary: Effect Accuracy, HP%

Build 2: Damage Mage

Balanced build that allows Cleopatra to deal significant damage while still providing support.

Equipment

  • Weapon: Arcane Staff (Magic + Crit)
  • Helmet: Battle Crown (Magic + Attack)
  • Armor: Mystic Robe (Magic + Penetration)
  • Boots: Magic Boots (Magic + Speed)
  • Ring: Power Ring (Magic + Crit Damage)
  • Accessory: Spellbook (Magic + Cooldown)

Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Classic Balanced

Alexander (Tank)
Cleopatra (Support)
Leonidas (DPS)

Strategy: The gold standard team. Alexander tanks, Cleopatra heals/buffs, Leonidas deals massive damage.

Team 2: Double DPS

Marcus (Tank)
Cleopatra
Artemis (DPS)

Strategy: Maximum damage output with Cleopatra keeping everyone alive. Great for quick clears.

Team 3: Mage Team

Alexander
Cleopatra
Merlin (Mage)

Strategy: Double mage composition for burst damage. Cleopatra sustains while Merlin nukes.

Battle Strategies

PvE (Dragon Battles)

  • Position Cleopatra in the back row for safety
  • Use ultimate right after dragon's big attack
  • Keep her energy high for constant healing
  • Pair with tanks that can protect her

PvP (Arena)

  • Focus on outlasting enemy teams with healing
  • Buff your highest damage dealer
  • Save ultimate for emergency situations
  • Target enemy supports first with attacks

Alliance Wars

  • Essential for rally attacks - always include
  • Her buffs significantly increase rally damage
  • Use healing to sustain prolonged sieges
  • Protect her at all costs in defense

Upgrade Priority

Priority Aspect Reason
High Star Level Unlocks healing bonuses and increases magic power
High Ultimate Skill Main healing and buffing ability
High Magic Stats Increases both healing and damage
Medium Energy Regen Allows more frequent ultimate usage
Low Defense Stats Should be protected by tanks instead

Player Tips

Tip 1: Cleopatra is the #1 priority hero for free-to-play players. Save all scrolls for her events.

Tip 2: Her attack buff stacks multiplicatively. Coordinate with alliance to maximize damage output.

Tip 3: In KvK, always bring Cleopatra. Her healing can turn the tide of large battles.

Tip 4: Invest in energy regen items. More ultimates = more healing = more wins.
