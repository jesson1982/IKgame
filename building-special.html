Special Buildings Guide

Essential special buildings that provide critical functions for your kingdom's growth and survival.

Special Buildings Overview

These buildings provide unique functions essential for progression. Prioritize them carefully!

🏰 Town Hall

The most important building! Town Hall level determines the maximum level of all other buildings.

  • Function: Unlocks building upgrades and features
  • Priority: ALWAYS upgrade first
  • Unlocks: Higher building caps, new features
  • Strategy: Never upgrade other buildings beyond Town Hall level

⚠️ Critical

Town Hall is the bottleneck for all progression. Always prioritize Town Hall upgrades above everything else!

🏥 Hospital

Hospitals heal wounded troops after battles. Higher levels increase healing capacity and speed.

  • Function: Heal wounded troops automatically
  • Capacity: Increases with level
  • Healing Speed: Faster at higher levels
  • Priority: High - prevents troop deaths

💡 Hospital Tip

Keep hospital capacity higher than your army size. Wounded troops are better than dead troops!

🔬 Research Lab

Research Lab unlocks technologies that boost all aspects of your kingdom - economy, military, and defense.

  • Function: Research permanent upgrade technologies
  • Tech Trees: Economy, Military, Defense
  • Priority: Very High - permanent bonuses
  • Strategy: Focus on economy tech first, then military

🏪 Marketplace

Marketplace allows trading resources with other players and purchasing special items.

  • Function: Resource trading and special purchases
  • Trade Limit: Increases with level
  • Exchange Rate: Better rates at higher levels
  • Priority: Medium - useful but not critical
